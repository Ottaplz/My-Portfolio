How it works

This website is designed to return a suggestion of one or more Dota 2 heroes to you based on certain aspects or traits that you require in your game.

The more required elements you choose the less results you will receive so be careful not to be too specific!

The off-role checkbox will only do something if a main role is already selected, otherwise your request will go through as per normal without providing any off-role options.
