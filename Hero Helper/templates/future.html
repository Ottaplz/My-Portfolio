The Future of Hero Helper

Planned extra features and QOL improvements to Hero Helper in the future
  • Hero profile pictures next to their respective results
  • Including hero/item synergies as an additional filter to find heroes based on commonly built items
  • Incorporating Dota 2 client API for the ability to filter results based on currently banned heroes
  • Update hero traits to most recent update patch (Currently about 12 months old kekw)
  • Update with newly released heroes (Ringmaster, Kez)
  • Add the ability to select multiple traits
  • Functionality for the results to be dynamically returned upon adding new requirements to forms. This should remove the need to submit a single form and make the user experience much easier to narrow down heroes according to your requirements quickly.
